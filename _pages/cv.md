Research Interests and Skills
=====
* Topical: coordination and collaboration in software teams that are existing or newly formed, working in an extermely compessed time scale, building software or non-softwae artifacts in a distributed, decentralized world
* Disciplinary: computer-supported collaborative work (CSCW), science and technology studies (STS), human-computer interaction (HCI)
* Theoretical: socio-technical systems, communities of practice
* Methodological:
  * Ethnography: interviews, participant and non-participant observations
  * Archival analysis: individual and group-based qualitative coding
  * Quantitative collection and analysis of log and trace data with Python, SQL, and R
  * User interface evaluation including experimental design, A/B testing and analsyis
  * Case study and mixed-methods approach to answer complex socio-technical questions using all of the above methods
